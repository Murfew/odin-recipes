Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-comsuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the reciper below.

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotte mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according tothe recipe instuctions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
